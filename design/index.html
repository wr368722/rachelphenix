Alignment

Mixbook

The Mixbook website appears to have great alignment. In both desktop and mobile view, the website is properly and beautifully designed. The alignment makes it easy for the viewers eye to gracefully go from the top of the screen to the bottom. Even the images that slide through the screen have matching horizontal lines that flow really well.
