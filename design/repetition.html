Repetition

adidias

The adidas website always has such amazing graphic design. The repetition in this home page design is clear. The diagonal white lines in the background, and in the show design. That repetition causes your eye to easily flow from one thing to the other. They also repeat the horizontal white lines in the buttons in the middle with the arrows.
